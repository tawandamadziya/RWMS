About Us



Welcome to Retail Waste management Services (RWMS)

At RWMS, we are committed to revolutionising waste management for businesses in Cape Town and the rest of the Western Cape Province.Our mission is to provide comprehensive, efficient,and environmentally responsible waste management solutions tailored to the unique needs of retailers, wholesalers, and other waste- generating businesses.

Who We Are

Retail Waste management Services (RWMS) is a dedicated waste management company based in Cape Town, specialising in servicing the retail and wholesale sectors. RWMS is accreditation by the City of Cape Town(Accreditation Number - SWMAC24/000065).Our team at RWMS comprises exprienced professionals passionate about Sustainability and waste reduction. We understand the challenges businesses face in managing waste effectively and are here to provide innovative and customized solutions.
